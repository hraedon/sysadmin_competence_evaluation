The audit log identifies the cause: two distinct Entra Connect sync accounts are alternating writes on the same user objects. The entries show Sync_SYNCSRV01_8f4a2c@contoso.onmicrosoft writing the Finance/+1-555-0198 values, then Sync_SYNCSRV02_3b7e19@contoso.onmicrosoft overwriting them with Operations/+1-555-0142, on a ~2 minute delay, repeating every sync cycle.

The Entra Connect Health server list confirms what the audit log implies: both SYNCSRV01 and SYNCSRV02 are listed as Active. Two Active instances is not a supported configuration. Entra Connect requires exactly one Active instance writing to the tenant at a time. When two are Active, each writes from its own AD source on its own schedule and continuously overwrites the other's changes.

SYNCSRV02 was stood up three days ago during the Meridian acquisition. It was deployed as an Active instance rather than a Staging instance, which is the mistake.

The fix: put SYNCSRV02 in Staging mode. In Staging mode it continues to sync from acquired.local locally but does not write to Entra. This stops the attribute reversions immediately.
