The important qualification:

It does not confirm that Rachel Okonkwo personally sent it, or that anyone at Meridian Consulting with legitimate authority sent it.

A compromised SendGrid account, a malicious insider at Meridian, or an attacker who had obtained Meridian's SendGrid API key would produce identical authentication results — SPF pass, DKIM pass, DMARC pass — because they would be sending through the same authorised infrastructure. The headers authenticate the relay chain, not the human originator.

For a routine business email from a known contact this distinction is academic. For a message arriving unexpectedly from a new contact requesting action on an attachment, it is the relevant limit of what header analysis can tell you. J.
