Change Specification: Computer-Auth Template Validity Extension

Proposed change: Extend the validity period on the Computer-Auth certificate template from 1 year to 2 years.

Scope: This change will affect all domain-joined computers that use the Computer-Auth certificate template.

Implementation steps:
1. Open the Certificate Templates console on the CA server
2. Locate the Computer-Auth template
3. Open properties and navigate to the General tab
4. Change the validity period from 1 year to 2 years
5. Click OK to save

Testing: Test on a few workstations before deploying widely.

Rollback plan: If issues occur, revert the template validity period back to 1 year.
