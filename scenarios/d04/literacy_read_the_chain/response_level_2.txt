The certificate that is causing the validation failure is Certificate 2: CN=CorpIssuing-CA-01.

While the site certificate (intranet.corp.example.com) is valid until November 2026, the Issuing CA certificate that signed it has a "Valid To" date of 2026-03-23. Since today is March 23, 2026, the intermediate certificate has expired today.

This is why the leaf certificate's expiry date is not the issue — a browser cannot validate the chain if any certificate in that path (from the root down to the leaf) is expired. The client's browser is likely displaying an error like "NET::ERR_CERT_DATE_INVALID" or a more specific message about the certification path not being within its validity period.
