Title: ProcessingWorker service severely degraded since go-live

Description:
ProcessingWorker went live on 2026-03-16 after three days of testing. During testing
it processed the message queue without issues — latency p95 was under 800ms and the
queue stayed below 100 messages. In production the service degraded within two hours
of go-live. Processing latency is now consistently 8–12 seconds p95. The queue is
backing up and has reached 4,200 messages as of this morning. The service hasn't been
changed since testing. We need this resolved today.

— Dev lead, AppServices team
