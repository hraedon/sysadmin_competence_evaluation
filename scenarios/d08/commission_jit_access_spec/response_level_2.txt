We cannot grant permanent local administrator access due to our security policy. Instead, I will set up a process where developers can request temporary admin access when they need to troubleshoot SQLSRV-01. When a dev needs access, they will submit a ticket, and we will manually add them to the Local Administrators group on the server. We will then remove them from the group once their work is finished or at the end of the day. This way, they have the rights they need without having them all the time.
