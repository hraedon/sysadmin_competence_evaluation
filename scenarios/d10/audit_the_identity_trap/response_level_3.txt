There is a major "Identity Trap" in this architecture. Because all administrative identities are synced from on-premises AD to Entra ID via Entra Connect, a compromise of the primary domain would likely lead to a total lockout from the Azure portal. This means the "off-site" backups would be inaccessible exactly when they are needed most. 

While the Tier 3 tapes are immune to this because they are physical and don't rely on Entra ID, the RTO/RPO for monthly tapes is much worse than the Azure backups. Losing 30 days of data and the time to retrieve the tapes would be a major blow to the business. The Azure backups are "off-site" for data, but they are "on-site" for identity, which is a critical failure. This architecture does not provide true isolation for the Tier 1 and Tier 2 tiers.
