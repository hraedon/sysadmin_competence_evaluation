== INVESTIGATION NOTES: INC-2026-0411 ==
Investigator:  D. Walsh (Junior Administrator)
Date:          2026-03-23
Incident:      Possible unauthorized access to Accounting share on FILESRV01
               (reported 2026-03-23; alleged incident window: 2026-03-02, approx. 14:00–16:00)

BACKGROUND
HR notified IT that a former employee account (T.Nakamura, terminated 2026-03-01)
may have been used to access the Accounting file share on 2026-03-02. The account
was scheduled for disabling 5 business days post-termination per policy, which
would place the disable date at 2026-03-08. Investigators want to determine whether
T.Nakamura's account accessed the Accounting share on 2026-03-02.

INVESTIGATION STEPS TAKEN

Step 1: Reviewed Windows Security log on FILESRV01 for 2026-03-02, 14:00–16:00.
  Filter applied: Account Name = nakamura
  Result: No logon events (4624) or logon failure events (4625) found for nakamura
          in the specified time window.

Step 2: Confirmed T.Nakamura's account status.
  Account: CORP\t.nakamura
  Status on 2026-03-02: Active (not yet disabled per policy)
  Disabled date: 2026-03-08 09:14 (per IT service desk ticket)

CONCLUSION
No logon events for T.Nakamura's account were found in the FILESRV01 Security log
during the alleged incident window. Based on this evidence, T.Nakamura's account
was not used to access FILESRV01 or the Accounting share on 2026-03-02.
